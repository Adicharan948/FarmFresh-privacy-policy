Privacy Policy - FarmFresh

Last updated: March 2025

Welcome to FarmFresh, a farmer-to-consumer marketplace app. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to protecting your personal information.

This Privacy Policy outlines:

  • What data we collect and how we use it.
  • How we store and secure your data.
  • Your rights regarding your data.
  • How to contact us for concerns or inquiries.

1. Data We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you register or use our services, we may collect:

  • Full name
  • Phone number (used for OTP login)
  • Email address
  • Location data (for nearby farmers and deliveries)

1.2 Usage Data

  • Device and app usage information
  • Activity timestamps
  • Crash logs and diagnostics

1.3 Payment Information

Payments are processed securely through third-party payment gateways. FarmFresh does not store your card or UPI details.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Authenticate users securely
  • Connect consumers with nearby farmers
  • Process orders and deliveries
  • Improve app performance and services

3. Data Security

  • Secure authentication methods
  • Encrypted data transmission
  • Restricted access controls

4. Your Rights

  • Access and update your information
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Delete your account anytime

5. Third-Party Services

  • Payment gateways
  • Location services
  • Notification services

6. Children's Privacy

FarmFresh is not intended for children under the age of 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here.
