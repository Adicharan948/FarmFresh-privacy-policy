Privacy Policy – FarmFresh

Last Updated: March 2025

FarmFresh is an Android-based farmer-to-consumer marketplace application. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal and transactional data.

1. Information We Collect

  • Name, email address, and phone number (used for OTP login)
  • Location details for nearby farmer discovery and delivery
  • Order details, product listings, and transaction history
  • App usage data for performance improvement

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Secure user authentication and OTP verification
  • Connecting farmers with consumers
  • Order processing, delivery coordination, and notifications
  • Improving app functionality and user experience

3. Payments & Transactions

All payments are processed through secure third-party payment gateways. FarmFresh does not store any card, bank, or UPI details on its servers.

4. Data Security

  • OTP-based authentication
  • Encrypted data transmission
  • Restricted database and API access

5. Account Deletion & Data Retention

  • Users can request account deletion at any time
  • All personal data will be permanently deleted upon request
  • Order records may be retained temporarily for legal or accounting purposes

6. Third-Party Services

  • Payment gateways
  • Location services
  • Notification services

7. Children’s Privacy

FarmFresh is not intended for users under the age of 13.

8. Policy Updates

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
